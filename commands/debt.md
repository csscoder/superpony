---
description: Surface ponytail: shortcuts and their upgrade paths as a debt ledger
argument-hint: [path, optional — defaults to repo]
---

Use the `superpony:ponytail-debt` skill to scan `{{args}}` (default: the repo) for `ponytail:` markers and other deliberate simplifications, and list them as a debt ledger: each entry = location, the current ceiling, and the named upgrade path. Flag any shortcut that has reached its ceiling and now needs the upgrade.
